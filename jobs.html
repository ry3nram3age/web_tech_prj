Cybersecurity Specialist

Breif Description

  • Job Title: Cybersecurity Specialist
  • Company: Sydney CyberSec
  • Location: Melbourne HQ
  • Salary Range: $90,000 - $130,000 per year (based on experience)

Job Description:

Sydney CyberSec, an innovative and rapidly expanding cybersecurity firm, is looking for a skilled and detail-oriented Cybersecurity Specialist to join our growing team. As a Cybersecurity Specialist, you will play a critical role in protecting our clients' digital assets by identifying vulnerabilities, implementing security protocols, and responding to security incidents. You will work with cutting-edge technologies to design, monitor, and maintain robust security infrastructures, ensuring the highest level of security across our systems and networks.

Key Job Responsibilities:

  • Security Analysis & Vulnerability Management: Perform regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify weaknesses within systems and networks.
  • Incident Response: Lead and coordinate responses to security breaches and incidents, ensuring quick resolution and comprehensive root cause analysis.
  • Security Architecture & Design: Collaborate with IT teams to design and implement secure networks, systems, and infrastructure.
  • Risk Management: Assess security risks and develop mitigation strategies, ensuring the company's compliance with security regulations and best practices.
  • Monitoring & Threat Intelligence: Monitor security alerts and use threat intelligence to proactively detect and defend against security breaches, malware, and cyberattacks.
  • Security Audits & Reporting: Conduct security audits and prepare detailed reports for management and clients, including recommendations for improvements.
  • Employee Training: Educate employees on best security practices and help implement security awareness training programs across the organization.
  • Compliance: Ensure the organization meets industry security standards and regulatory compliance requirements (e.g., GDPR, ISO 27001, NIST, etc.).

Qualifications Required:

Essential:
  1. Experience: Minimum of 3 years of hands-on experience in cybersecurity, with a proven track record in security monitoring, incident response, and vulnerability management.
  2. Technical Knowledge: Strong understanding of network protocols, firewalls, encryption, intrusion detection systems, and security tools (e.g., SIEM, IDS/IPS, antivirus software, etc.).
  3. Certifications: Relevant cybersecurity certifications, such as Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), CompTIA Security+, or equivalent.
  4. Security Tools: Experience with security tools and platforms like Splunk, Wireshark, Nessus, or others for vulnerability scanning and incident response.
  5. Analytical Skills: Strong problem-solving abilities with the ability to analyze and address complex security issues.
  6. Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to explain complex security concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
  7. Attention to Detail: Ability to identify potential security threats and propose solutions with precision and care.
Preferable:
  1. Experience in Cybersecurity Operations: Experience in a Security Operations Center (SOC) or with advanced threat detection tools is highly desirable.
  2. Advanced Certifications: Advanced certifications, such as Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Cloud Security Professional (CCSP), or Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP), are a plus.
  3. Programming Knowledge: Familiarity with scripting languages such as Python, PowerShell, or Bash for automating security processes.
  4. Cloud Security: Experience securing cloud infrastructures (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP) and understanding of cloud security principles.
  5. Knowledge of Compliance Standards: In-depth understanding of regulatory frameworks such as PCI-DSS, HIPAA, or SOC 2.
Languages Required:
  • English: Fluent (written and spoken)
  • Additional languages (e.g., Spanish, Mandarin, etc.) would be considered a bonus but are not required.

Hiring Team Manager

Breif Description

Job Title: Hiring Team Manager Company: Sydney's CyberSec Location: Melbourne HQ Salary Range: $85,000 - $110,000 per year (based on experience) Job Description: Sydney CyberSec, a leading cybersecurity firm, is seeking an experienced Hiring Team Manager to lead our recruitment efforts. This role will oversee the full recruitment lifecycle, managing a small but efficient team of recruiters, and collaborating with department heads to ensure that Sydney CyberSec continues to attract, hire, and retain top-tier cybersecurity talent. You will be responsible for setting strategic hiring goals, improving recruitment processes, and ensuring that the best candidates are selected to strengthen our company's capabilities in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.

Key Job Responsibilities:

  • Recruitment Strategy Development: Collaborate with senior leadership to define recruitment strategies that align with company goals and business needs.
  • Team Leadership & Development: Lead and mentor a team of recruiters, providing coaching, feedback, and development opportunities.
  • Full-Cycle Recruiting: Oversee the recruitment process from job requisition to candidate offer, including sourcing, interviewing, and negotiating job offers.
  • Collaborative Hiring: Work closely with hiring managers and department leads to understand staffing requirements and identify ideal candidate profiles.
  • Process Improvement: Continuously assess and improve the recruitment process, implementing best practices and ensuring a positive candidate experience.
  • Diversity & Inclusion: Champion diversity and inclusion within the recruitment process, ensuring a fair and unbiased approach to hiring.
  • Reporting & Metrics: Track and report on key hiring metrics such as time-to-fill, quality of hire, and candidate satisfaction.

Qualifications Required:

Essential:
  • Experience: Minimum of 5 years of experience in recruitment, with at least 2 years in a leadership or managerial role within a fast-paced environment.
  • Industry Knowledge: Strong understanding of the cybersecurity industry and its unique recruitment needs.
  • Leadership Skills: Proven experience in managing and developing a team of recruiters.
  • Recruitment Software: Proficiency in using applicant tracking systems (ATS) and other recruitment tools (e.g., LinkedIn Recruiter, Greenhouse, etc.).
  • Communication: Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to engage and influence stakeholders at all levels of the organization.
  • Project Management: Ability to handle multiple recruitment projects simultaneously and meet deadlines.
Preferable:
  • Recruitment-related certifications (e.g., CIR, AIRS, or similar) would be a plus.
  • Experience in Cybersecurity Recruiting: Experience specifically recruiting for technical and cybersecurity roles is highly desirable.
  • Familiarity with HR analytics or metrics-driven recruitment strategies to improve hiring decisions.

Languages Required:

  • English: Fluent (written and spoken).
  • Additional languages (e.g., Spanish, Mandarin, etc.) would be considered a bonus but are not required.
